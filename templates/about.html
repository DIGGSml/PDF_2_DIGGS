About the Project

The DIGGS 2.6 Converter was developed for the 2026 DIGGS Hackathon. Our mission is to bridge the gap between legacy PDF borehole logs and the modern data interchange standard, Data Interchange for Geotechnical and Geoenvironmental Specialists (DIGGS).

What is DIGGS?

DIGGS is an XML-based schema for the transfer of geotechnical and geoenvironmental data. It allows for the seamless exchange of data between different software packages and organizations.

Version 2.6

This tool specifically targets DIGGS version 2.6, ensuring compliance with the commonly used standards in the industry today.
